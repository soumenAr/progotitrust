A Message from Rinku Ghosh

At Progoti Development & Welfare Trust, we believe that true development begins with empathy and grows through collective action. Every child we support, every woman we empower, and every family we stand beside strengthens our resolve to keep moving forward together.

Our mission is rooted in dignity. We work to ensure that communities have access to opportunity, knowledge, health, and hope. We do not see people as beneficiaries; we see them as partners in building a better future.

I invite all citizens, volunteers, and institutions to walk with us in this journey. Together, let us create a society where compassion leads policy, inclusion shapes growth, and progress reaches every doorstep.

"When we rise by lifting others, progress becomes meaningful and lasting."
