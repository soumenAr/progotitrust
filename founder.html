The Person Behind the Mission

Meet Founder Subrata Saradar and the dream that continues to guide this work.

Subrata Saradar's Dream for the NGO

Founder Subrata Saradar brings 20 years of service in the social sector, with sustained grassroots engagement and community leadership.

He has been actively involved with RSS, Salute Tiranga, and Bharatiya Janata Party (as Bistarak).

His dream is to build a society where every child has access to learning, every family can reach quality healthcare, and every youth can earn with dignity through skills and opportunities.

He believes real change happens when communities are trusted, local leaders are empowered, and development is designed with people, not for people.

"Our work is not charity. It is a shared commitment to dignity, equity, and opportunity for all."
