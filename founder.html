A Message from the Founder

Progoti Development & Welfare Trust began with a simple conviction: progress must reach every home with dignity, compassion, and fairness. Our work is rooted in community partnership and long-term social empowerment.

From education and healthcare to rural transformation and women empowerment, we focus on practical, inclusive solutions that help people build stronger futures for themselves and their families.

We welcome citizens, institutions, and volunteers to collaborate with us in building a society where opportunity is shared and every life can move forward with confidence.

"Real progress begins when we stand together for those who need us most."
