    Our Chairperson

    Guiding our vision and initiatives is our esteemed Chairperson Smt. Nita Mukesh Ambani, whose leadership and vision inspire us to reach new heights. Her commitment to philanthropy and societal well-being sets the tone for Reliance Foundation's impactful contributions.

    Our Story

    Our journey is one of dedication, passion, and unwavering commitment. Founded with a vision to make a meaningful difference, Reliance Foundation's story is shaped by the collective efforts of a team dedicated to fostering positive change and creating lasting impact.

    Our Impact & Reach

    Driven by a commitment to measurable impact, Reliance Foundation's initiatives have touched the lives of millions across the country. Our extensive reach ensures that our efforts have a far-reaching and lasting effect on communities.

    Annual Report

    Explore our Annual Reports to gain insights into the progress, achievements, and challenges faced throughout the year. These reports provide a comprehensive overview of our initiatives and their impact on the ground.

    Awards

    Our dedication and impact have been acknowledged through various accolades and awards. These recognitions serve as a testament to our commitment to excellence and our continuous efforts to contribute positively to society.

    What We do

    Reliance Foundation engages in a wide spectrum of activities, from healthcare initiatives to educational programs, rural transformation projects, and disaster response. Our multifaceted approach is aimed at creating holistic and sustainable solutions.

    Rural Transformation

    Empowering some of the most marginalised communities across rural India with innovative and sustainable solutions since 2010.

    Education

    Education is at the heart of Reliance Foundation’s vision for India and central to the country’s social and economic progress.

    Health

    We believe that quality and affordable healthcare is crucial not only for communities but also for the economic and social well-being of India.

    Sports For Development

    At the heart of our sports initiatives, the goal is to cultivate lifelong learning, build character, and foster leadership skills among India's youth.

    Disaster Management

    Reliance Foundation’s Disaster Management programme supports vulnerable hotspots in building safer and disaster-resilient communities.

    Women Empowerment

    Empowered women mean a stronger and a better world and we are committed to empowering women to play a catalytic role in bringing change.

    Arts, Culture & Heritage

    Reliance Foundation is constantly reimagining ways to protect and promote Indian Arts, Culture & Heritage.

    Environment & Animal Welfare

    Creating a better world for all beings through Vantara — a landmark initiative for wildlife rescue, care and conservation, and community-linked sustainability across India

    Urban Renewal

    The Urban Renewal initiatives of Reliance Foundation strive to enrich and improve public spaces to improve the quality of life and infrastructure in cities.
